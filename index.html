About

Brian Aston is a gaming enthusiast looking to harness his passion for fun into a creative force. A deep love of games and puzzles drove him to become a budding game developer, and now a software engineer. He graduated from University of California, Santa Cruz with a bachelors in Computer Science: Game Design. He has helped engineer several games and is now seeking to expand his knowledge into web design and engineering.


Brian has extensive knowledge of the Unity game engine and has created several projects in Unity as detailed below. Additionally, he has former web development experience after working for The Green Sheet.
